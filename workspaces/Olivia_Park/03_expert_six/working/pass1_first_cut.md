### 1. Name and Title

**Josh Bernoff** — Author of *Writing Without Bullshit* and *Build a Better Business Book*; business writing coach; executive ghostwriting and corporate writing critic.

### 2. Selection Rationale — Why This One of the Six

Bernoff is the strongest fit for disciplined executive writing, editorial redlining, clarity, and anti-bullshit corporate communication. His work is especially useful for Olivia’s critic mode: identifying vague abstraction, inflated claims, weak logic, passive corporate prose, and writing that sounds polished but says little. Bernoff’s methodology is unusually decomposable. His work repeatedly explains why business writing fails: because organizations substitute tone, polish, and process for direct claims, evidence, and reader value. For Olivia, his body of work is valuable as a redlining model: cut what does not inform, support, clarify, or move the argument forward.
