None.

- **LinkedIn-specific executive ghostwriting** is sufficiently covered by Koshy and Moore, but if LinkedIn becomes the dominant format, Katy Chrisler would be the strongest backup addition.
- **Pure AI-search / GEO mechanics** are adequately covered for this role, but Metehan Yesilyurt is the best alternate if machine-readable discoverability becomes a higher priority than founder-led voice and editorial judgment.
